Cookie Policy

Last Updated: June 8, 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the site owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off.
  • Analytics Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website.
  • Preference Cookies: These enable the website to remember information that changes the way the site behaves or looks.
  • Marketing Cookies: These are used to track visitors across websites.

3. Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

To learn more about how to manage cookies, visit aboutcookies.org.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page.
